Easy Pro Forma

Easily run the numbers on a real estate investment. On-site or on the go.

Features

Instant Investment Metrics

Instantly see the metrics you care about including cap rate, net operating incoming, cash on cash return, before tax cash flow, and gross rent multiplier. See what matters, not what doesn't. If you want to take a deeper dive, easily jump into the details with additional metrics such as monthly debt payment, total acquisition cost, and rent per square foot.

Edit and Save Expense Assumptions

Easily tweak expense figures and instantly see how it impacts the investment metrics. With Premium, you can save these assumptions as templates for future use - just add your inputs, choose your expense template, and see if its a deal.

Share the Numbers

With Premium, you can easily share your numbers with partners, colleagues, and clients. With the click of a button you can let others see the full slate of numbers.
